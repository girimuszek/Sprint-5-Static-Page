A TheBoys Story

Team Members: Kacper Zalewski

Introduction

A TheBoys Story will be an interactive story game that can be played in Terminal/Powershell after an user installs it. They can choose what can happen in the story by entering a series of numbers, answering yes or no questions, etc.

This application will be composed purely using Python and on a Terminal or Powershell. It will very significantly make use of functions, plus a lot of other very useful Python tools.

This proposal was last updated on 11/6/22

Feature List

  • A story with multiple endings
  • A lot of dialogue for the story

Future Feature List

  • A proper UI
  • Images or miniature animations made using GMOD to accompany the game's story
  • More story options
